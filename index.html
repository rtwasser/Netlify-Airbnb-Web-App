How-To Guide

☕ Espresso Machine

Bosch 800 Series: Water tank is on the right side. Fresh beans are in the cupboard directly above the machine. Run rinse cycle after use.

🔥 Outdoor Fire Table

1. Open door, turn propane cap left. 2. Turn knob left, press spark button. 3. Hold knob in while sparking to ignite. **Always turn propane fully off (to the right) when finished.**

🤖 Robot Vacuum

Roborock: Press Power to start whole-home clean. No need to empty; there is enough space for your stay.

🧺 Laundry

You are welcome to use the washing machine for your own clothes as needed. No need to wash bedding or towels upon checkout.
