Thoughtful Amenities

Morning Ritual

The Bosch 800 Series Espresso machine makes over 30 varieties of coffee. Please check the water tank on the left and ensure the bean hopper is full.

Rest & Recharge

The bedroom features a King-size bed with hotel-standard linens. The Flex room offers a Murphy bed or a dedicated workspace with a mountain view.

Ambiance

Use the remote for the living room's electric fireplace. On the patio, the gas fire table provides warmth for evening city-watching.

The View

North-facing views capture the North Shore mountains and the working port. Note: Earplugs are provided in the nightstand for the freight train line below.

Respecting the Space

  • No parties, events, or commercial filming.
  • No smoking or vaping on the balcony or inside.
