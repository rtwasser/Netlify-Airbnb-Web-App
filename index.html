Appliances

How-To Guide

Espresso Machine

Bosch 800 Series: Water tank is on the **right side**. Fresh beans are in the cupboard directly above the machine. Run rinse cycle after use.

Outdoor Fire Table

1. Open door, turn propane cap left. 2. Turn knob left, press spark button. 3. Hold knob in for a few seconds to ignite.

Robot Vacuum

Press Power to start whole-home clean. Press Home to return to base. If stuck, clear debris from underneath and press Power to resume.

Samsung Bespoke Vacuum

Located in the den. Lift off base, use touch controls. Return to base after use; it will automatically empty the dirt.

House Rules

Comfort & Care

  • Shoes Off: Please remove your shoes when entering the home.
  • Balcony Safety: Do not leave children unattended on the balcony at any time.
  • Climate: Each room has a thermostat. Comfortable range is 20-22°C. On hot days, ensure AC tubes are properly inserted in the window.
  • Kitchen: Tap water is safe to drink. A Brita filter is in the fridge, and ice trays are in the mini-fridge freezer.
  • Supplies: Feel free to use laundry detergent and cleaning products. Please use only toilet paper in the toilet; other items go in the bin.
Safety First

In Case of Emergency

Emergency Services: Dial 911 (Police, Fire, Ambulance).

Nearest Hospital: St. Paul's Hospital (Downtown).

Fire Extinguishers: Under the kitchen sink, and a larger one in the building hallway to the left of our suite exit.

Building Safety: Emergency stairs are located directly to the left in the hallway. Use fire doors as marked.

Departure

Checkout Checklist

  • Check-out is at 12:00 PM.
  • Turn off all lights, appliances, and fireplaces.
  • Linens & Dishes: Our cleaners will handle these. However, if you'd like to help, feel free to run a load of dishes and strip the sheets.
  • Left Items: Any items left behind will be placed in the hallway. We will notify you of lost items; pick-up or shipping (at guest's expense) can be arranged based on availability.
  • Contact: All messaging must be through the Airbnb app. We reply promptly.
