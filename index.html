Appliances

How-To Guide

☕ Espresso Machine

Bosch 800 Series: Water tank is on the **right side**. Fresh beans are in the cupboard directly above the machine. Run rinse cycle after use.

🔥 Outdoor Fire Table

1. Open door, turn propane cap left. 2. Turn knob left, press spark button. 3. Hold knob in while sparking to ignite. **Turn propane fully right to close.**

🤖 Robot Vacuum

Roborock: Press Power to start. Returns to base on its own. No need to empty; there is enough space for your stay.

🧹 Bespoke Vacuum

Samsung: Located in den. Lift off base, use touch controls. Returns to base to empty automatically. No manual emptying needed.

House Rules

Comfort & Care

  • Shoes: Please remove shoes inside the home.
  • Children: Do not leave children unattended on the balcony.
  • Sofa: Headrests are adjustable; sofa pulls out for lounging.
  • Climate: Thermostats in each room. Comfort: 20-22°C. Ensure AC tubes are in window on hot days.
  • Utilities: Tap water is safe. Brita in fridge; ice in mini-fridge freezer. Only TP in toilet (bin for all else).
  • Supplies: Feel free to use laundry detergent, cleaning products, and house supplies.
Safety First

In Case of Emergency

Emergency Services: Dial 911 (Police, Fire, Ambulance).

Nearest Hospital: St. Paul's Hospital (Downtown).

Fire Extinguishers: Under kitchen sink, and in building hallway left of suite exit.

Exit: Emergency stairs directly to the left in the hallway. Use fire doors as marked.

Departure

Checkout Checklist

  • Check-out is at 12:00 PM.
  • Laundry: Use machine for your clothes as needed. No need to wash linens/towels on exit.
  • Cleaning: Cleaners handle dishes/linens, but feel free to run dishes or strip sheets to help!
  • Ensure all lights, appliances, and fireplaces are off.
  • Crucial: FIRMLY PUSH/PULL DOOR SHUT on your way out.
  • Leave fob on the kitchen counter and message us via Airbnb app.
